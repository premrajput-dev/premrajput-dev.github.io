Who I am & what I do

I'm Prem Rajput, a Robotics and Autonomous Engineering student driven by one question: how do we make machines that understand the world?

I'm comfortable going from low-level microcontroller programming to high-level perception pipelines. I care about systems that are not just functional, but reliable and safe in the real world.
